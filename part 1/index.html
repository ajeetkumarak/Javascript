    • How to get from Junior to Senior Developer ?

            Although, there's not quick shortcut, here are few things  you can do to speed up the process:


  • Choose a job based on what can you learn there, not on money - grab the techinque from experienced person, And Boost your Experience.......

  • Ask questions about how things work and what are the recommended approaches - you can write code and finish tasks without understanding how it works and what's the best way to do it. Don't do that! Always read and ask about alternative solutions, recommendations and what can you improve.

  • Clean And Organized Code - it doesn't seem important, but it is! Read about designs patterns, SOLID Principles, look for examples of how are they sapplied in code, then do the same with your code. Always think about the next person who's going to work there after you, and make his/her life easier.

  • Self reflection - evaluate yourself from time to time , look at your old code, look at how much help you need from colleagues vs. last year, and so on. If you don't notice any improvements, look for a change.
